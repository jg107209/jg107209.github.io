Thanks for checking out my portfolio!

I'm recent transplant to the San Jose area from Columbus, Ohio. I graduated from the Scripps College of Communication at Ohio University in 2012 before embarking on a career in media. I’ve created pieces about all topics, in cities ranging from NYC to Chicago and San Francisco. I first began in digital media interning for the Miss Universe Organization, followed by producing a 24/7 stream of content for the City of Athens Government Channel. I recently served as Production Coordinator for Transvideo Studios, Silicon Valley’s largest video production company.

Currently, I serve as video producer for the Del Grande Dealer Group, the Bay Area's largest family-owned car dealership company. In just a year, I've helped create 100+ new videos for YouTube and broadcast, doubling the Video Team's output for the year prior.

In my spare time, I've directed several short films and written numerous full length screenplays. I’m a great listener, collaborater, creator and leader. I love brainstorming, so visit the contact page to share an idea or just say "What's up?"
